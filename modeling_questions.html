ABOUT FYNNS' AND IZABELLA MODELS
Established in 1984, IZABELLA Models is a leader in the development and placement of new models into the world's leading international modeling agencies. Models discovered by IZABELLA Models work around the world and have appeared in major magazines including Vogue, Glamour, Harpers Bazaar, Elle, Seventeen, Amica, Cosmopolitan, Marie Claire, Gear, Teen Vogue, Elle Girl, Self, Fitness, Modern Bride... and have worked for the fashion industry's leading clients and designers including Giorgio Armani, Chanel, Gucci, Givenchy, Versace, Versus, Dolce & Gabbana, Prada, Christian Lacroix, Bagley Mischka, Abercrombie and Fitch, Bill Blass, Calvin Klein, Valentino, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Yves. St. Laurent, Max Mara, Cartier, Tiffany and Co., Clairol, Pantene, Ponds, Revlon, Matrix, Neiman Marcus, Saks Fifth Avenue, Holt Renfrew and many, many more!

In the Summer of 2000, IZABELLA Models expanded the agency from female editorial models only to include male models, child models, petite and plus-size models. As IZABELLA Models is known within the industry for representing female editorial models only, it was determined that the new division should have its own unique identity. Hence the new division - Fynns' Models.

Yes. Fynns' Models is a proud member of the BBB and has received the BBB Online Reliability Seal for excellence.

WHAT IS THE MANAGEMENT DIVISION OF FYNNS' MODELS?
The Management Division of Fynns' Models represents experienced, professional models. Models in our Management Division have worked internationally in major markets such as New York, Los Angeles, Paris, Milan, Germany, Tokyo, Singapore, Taipei and Korea and have high-quality tearsheets in their books.

WHAT IS THE FREELANCE DIVISION OF FYNNS' MODELS?
The Freelance Division of Fynns' Models provides an entry level for new models who would like the opportunity to be seen by our clients and be considered for bookings. The requirements for the Freelance Division are much more relaxed than that of our management divisions. This is great for new models who may not have the resources to have professional photos taken, who are working on their portfolios or who would just like to see what the response to them will be before investing hundreds or thousands of dollars on photos, composite cards, modeling courses, model searches or conventions.

We understand that starting out and getting the attention of a reputable agency can be a difficult undertaking. We also understand that by giving a new model an opportunity we could have a future superstar on our hands. The Freelance Division is the best of both worlds, new models get incredible exposure to top clients and international agencies who visit our website every day and we have the opportunity to be involved in a budding career right from the start. We are pleased to provide this unique opportunity to new models.

HOW DO I MOVE FROM THE FREELANCE TO MANAGEMENT DIVISION?
All new models must begin in our Freelance Division (see above). To be represented by our Management Division models must have professional experience and at least 5 high quality international tearsheets (magazine or commercial work) in their books.

DO FREELANCE MODELS HAVE TO SIGN A CONTRACT?
No, freelance models are not required to sign a contract with the agency. As a freelance model you are free to work with other agencies and book jobs outside of the agency.

DO YOU REPRESENT CHILD MODELS, PETITE MODELS AND PLUS SIZE MODELS?
Yes, we represent child models from toddlers to pre-teen as well as petite models and plus size models.

DO YOU REPRESENT PARTS MODELS?
No, we do not represent parts models. (Parts models model hands, feet, legs and other body parts.)

IS THERE A FEE TO BE REPRESENTED BY YOUR AGENCY?
No. We do not charge fees for representation. We earn the standard 20% agency commission on work models perform through our agency.

IS THERE A FEE TO BE ON THE FYNNS' WEBSITE?
No. In the past, models were required to cover their own expenses such as website fees. But, recently Fynns' changed this policy and no longer charges models to post their photos on our website.

HOW WILL I KNOW WHEN A CLIENT IS INTERESTED IN ME OR WANTS TO BOOK ME FOR A JOB?
The agency will contact you by email first and then by telephone to provide you with the details of your booking.
Please note, that the agency will contact you only when we have a definite booking for you. The agency is simply too busy to contact models if a client has only expressed an interest in the model, has put them "on hold" or "on option" or if the agency is in the negotiation stage of the booking.

CAN YOU GUARANTEE THAT I WILL GET A JOB?
No. No agency can guarantee work. Ultimately, the client makes the final decision on whether or not a model is booked, not the agency.

WHAT IF I DON'T LIVE IN THE SAME CITY AS YOUR AGENCY?
That's ok. Our models live all over the world. That is the nature of our business. Our models travel constantly and we are used to working under these conditions.

WHAT IS A DIRECT BOOKING?
If you are not located in the same city where the job will take place or if the client books you based on your photos only, the booking is considered a "direct booking". This also means that the client will be responsible for your travel and accommodation expenses so you can perform the job.
