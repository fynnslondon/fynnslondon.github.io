Fynns' Models is the exciting new division of Izabella Models.

Izabella Models is considered one of the industry's most prestigious and exclusive modeling agencies representing only the very best fashion models.

If you are a new or experienced model who would like the benefits of working with a top modeling agency but on a non-exclusive basis then Fynns' Models is for you.

Fynns' Models makes it easier for models to break into the modeling business and be seen by top agents and clients since there are no age or height restrictions to be represented by Fynns'.

Fynns' Models represents female models, male models, child models, petite and plus size models of all ages.

With over 20 years experience representing models to international modeling agencies and clients in New York, Los Angeles, Paris, Milan, London, Australia, Spain, Greece, Tokyo, Singapore and Taipei you will receive the same exposure and consideration as models represented by our management division, Izabella Models.

Our models have appeared in all of the major magazines including Vogue, Glamour, Cosmopolitan, Elle, Flare, Marie Claire, Harper's Bazaar, Brides, Mademoiselle, Seventeen, Modern Bride, Italian Vogue, Teen Vogue, Elle Girl, Shape and Fitness and have worked for the industry's most prestigious clients including Giorgio Armani, Versace, Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, Gucci, Badgley Mishka, Prada, Dior, YSL, Donna Karan, Calvin Klein, Valentino, Christian LaCroix, Cartier, Tiffany and Co., Revlon, Clairol and Loreal.

Izabella Models and Fynns' Models are not modeling schools and we do not require that you register in modeling classes or pay fees to be represented by our agency. Our modeling agency receives a commission on work that you perform through our agency.
